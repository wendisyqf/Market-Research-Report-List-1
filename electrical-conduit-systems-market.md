<p><p>The electrical conduit systems market comprises two main types: rigid and flexible electrical conduit systems. Rigid electrical conduits, made from materials like metal or PVC, provide strong protection for wiring in fixed installations, ideal for permanent setups. In contrast, flexible electrical conduits are made from materials such as plastic or metal and offer adaptability for dynamic environments where movement is necessary, accommodating bends and turns. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safety and organization of electrical systems across various applications.</p></p>
