<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 test kits market includes various types of testing methods. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT) provide quick results, typically within minutes, using lateral flow technology to detect antibodies or antigens. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA) are laboratory-based tests that quantify antibodies in blood samples, offering higher sensitivity and specificity. Neutralization Assays evaluate the ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus, often used in research and vaccine studies. Each method plays a crucial role in diagnosing and managing the pandemic.</p></p>
