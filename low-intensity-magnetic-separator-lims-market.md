<p><p>Low Intensity Magnetic Separators (LIMS) are crucial in mineral processing, offering two primary types: dry and wet. Dry LIMS utilize air flows and magnetic fields to separate magnetic materials from non-magnetic ones without the use of water, making them suitable for arid conditions. In contrast, wet LIMS use a slurry of materials in a liquid medium, enhancing separation efficiency for fine particles. Both types are essential for improving the purity of ores and optimizing resource recovery in various industries.</p></p>
