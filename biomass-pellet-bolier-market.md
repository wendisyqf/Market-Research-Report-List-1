<p><p>The biomass pellet boiler market is segmented into three types based on automation levels. Fully-automatic pellet boilers require minimal user intervention, featuring automated feeding, ignition, and temperature control, making them highly convenient. Semi-automatic pellet boilers offer a balanced approach, needing some manual input for operations like fuel loading while still providing automated functionality. Manual pellet boilers, on the other hand, require complete user control for all processes, appealing to those seeking simplicity and lower upfront costs despite higher operational effort.</p></p>
