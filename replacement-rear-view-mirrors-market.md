<p><p>The Replacement Rear View Mirrors market includes two primary types: CCD cameras and CMOS cameras. Conversely, CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras are more energy-efficient, cost-effective, and enable faster image processing, which is advantageous for real-time applications. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for enhanced visibility and safety in vehicles, influencing consumer preferences in the automotive sector.</p></p>
