<p><p>The Advanced Perfusion System market is primarily segmented into two types: Single Roller Pump Heart Lung Machines and Double Roller Pump Heart Lung Machines. Single roller pump machines utilize one roller pump to circulate blood during surgeries, providing a simpler design and lower cost. In contrast, double roller pump machines use two roller pumps, offering enhanced flow rates and better blood management, making them suitable for more complex, high-risk procedures. Both systems are critical in cardiopulmonary bypass surgeries, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
