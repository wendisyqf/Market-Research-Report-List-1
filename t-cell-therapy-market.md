<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The T-Cell Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CAR T Cell Therapy</li><li>TCR Therapy</li><li>TIL Therapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The T-Cell Therapy market consists of several innovative approaches to cancer treatment. CAR T Cell Therapy utilizes genetically engineered T cells to target specific cancer antigens, offering personalized treatment. TCR Therapy also involves engineering T cells, but focuses on enhancing their ability to recognize and attack tumor cells via T-cell receptors. Tumor-Infiltrating Lymphocyte (TIL) Therapy involves extracting T cells from a patient's tumor, expanding them in the lab, and reinfusing them to target the cancer more effectively. Each method aims to boost the immune response against tumors.</p></p>
