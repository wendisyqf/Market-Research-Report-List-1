<p><p>Polyisoprene elastomers encompass various types, each with distinct properties and applications. 1,2-Polyisoprene is known for excellent elasticity, making it suitable for adhesives and coatings. Trans-1,4 Polyisoprene offers high-temperature stability, often used in automotive applications. Cis-1,4 Polyisoprene is renowned for its similar characteristics to natural rubber, utilized in tires and footwear. Lastly, 3,4 Polyisoprene features unique flexibility and strength, finding applications in specialized products like rubber bands and medical devices. Each type caters to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
