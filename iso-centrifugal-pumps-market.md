<p><p>The ISO centrifugal pumps market is categorized into horizontal and vertical types, each designed for specific applications. Horizontal ISO centrifugal pumps are typically used for transporting fluids in various industries due to their efficient design and easy maintenance. They operate with a horizontal shaft and are ideal for lower viscosity liquids. In contrast, vertical ISO centrifugal pumps are suited for applications where space is limited or where suction conditions are challenging, effectively handling higher viscosity fluids while maintaining a compact footprint.</p></p>
