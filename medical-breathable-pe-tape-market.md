<p><p>The medical breathable PE tape market is categorized primarily into two types: LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) and HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene). LDPE is known for its flexibility, softness, and conformability, making it suitable for wound dressing applications that require gentle adhesion and breathability. In contrast, HDPE is more rigid and offers superior strength and durability, often used in applications requiring a stronger hold. Both types are crucial for providing secure and comfortable solutions in medical settings while allowing air circulation.</p></p>
