<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic power meters are categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I meters are designed for basic optical power measurement, typically used for single-mode and multimode fibers in telecommunications. They provide essential functionality for field technicians. On the other hand, Type II meters offer advanced features, including wavelength-specific measurements, enhanced accuracy, and data logging capabilities. These meters cater to more complex applications in research, development, and high-precision installations, serving diverse needs in the fiber optics market.</p></p>
