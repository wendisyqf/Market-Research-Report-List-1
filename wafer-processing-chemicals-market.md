<p><p>The wafer processing chemicals market encompasses various essential materials used in semiconductor fabrication. Sputtering targets are utilized for thin-film deposition, while photoresists are photosensitive materials crucial for patterning during lithography. Wet-processing chemicals facilitate cleaning and etching processes, and CMP slurries are employed in chemical-mechanical polishing to achieve surface planarization. Copper plating chemicals are vital for interconnects in integrated circuits, and atmospheric and specialty gases are essential for various reactions and processes throughout semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring optimal performance and yield.</p></p>
